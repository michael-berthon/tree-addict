What's up with trees?

We all know what a tree is basically! Yet there are hundreds of types of trees!

Trees can grow different fruits, be dangerous or not, survive different climates, have different heights...

Yet, the biggest concern about trees is that they are dangerously threatened by human activity! Problem is, trees are actually what is keeping us alive in this world!

This is the reason Tree Addict exists: by knowing more about them, we can learn to love them and how to protect them!
